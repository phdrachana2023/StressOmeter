🌱 General Stress Relief Strategies
🧘 Practice 10 minutes of mindfulness or deep breathing each morning to calm your nervous system.
📵 Set firm work-life boundaries — avoid work emails and calls after your designated off hours.
🏃 Engage in at least 30 minutes of physical activity 4–5 days a week to reduce cortisol levels.
😴 Prioritize 7–8 hours of quality sleep every night. Poor sleep amplifies stress significantly.
🤝 Build peer support networks — regular conversations with trusted colleagues reduce isolation.
📓 Keep a stress journal — write down triggers, reactions, and coping strategies each day.
🌿 Take short nature breaks during the workday. Even 5–10 minutes outdoors resets focus.
🎯 Practice the 80/20 rule — focus energy on the 20% of tasks that produce 80% of results.
