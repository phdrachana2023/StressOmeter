Section B — OSI Basic Questionnaire (12 Questions)

Enter a number from 1 to 5 for each statement:
1 = Strongly Disagree  |  2 = Do Not Agree  |  3 = Not Sure  |  4 = Agree  |  5 = Strongly Agree
⚠ Questions marked FALSE KEY contain reverse-scored items — the system handles this automatically.

Q1.
I often have too much work to complete in the time available to me.
Sub-Scale I — Role Overload  |  Items: 1, 13, 25, 36, 44, 46  |  All true-keyed (6 items)
1=Strongly Disagree · 5=Strongly Agree
Q2.
I frequently feel unclear or confused about what is really expected of me in my job.
Sub-Scale II — Role Ambiguity  |  Items: 2, 14*, 26, 37  |  3 true + 1 false-keyed
1=Strongly Disagree · 5=Strongly Agree
Q3.
I get conflicting instructions or demands from different people at work (like boss, team, clients).
Sub-Scale III — Role Conflict  |  Items: 3, 15*, 27, 38*, 45  |  3 true + 2 false-keyed
1=Strongly Disagree · 5=Strongly Agree
Q4.
Workplace politics, favouritism, or unnecessary group pressures make my job harder than it should be.
Sub-Scale IV — Unreasonable Group & Political Pressures  |  Items: 4, 16, 28, 39  |  All true-keyed (4 items)
1=Strongly Disagree · 5=Strongly Agree
Q5.
I carry a lot of worry or responsibility for the safety, well-being, or success of other people (team members, students, customers, etc.).
Sub-Scale V — Responsibility for Persons  |  Items: 5, 17, 29  |  All true-keyed (3 items)
1=Strongly Disagree · 5=Strongly Agree
Q6.
My ideas, opinions, or inputs are rarely asked for or taken seriously in important decisions at work. FALSE KEY
Sub-Scale VI — Under Participation  |  Items: 6*, 18*, 30*, 40*  |  All false-keyed (4 items)
1=Strongly Disagree · 5=Strongly Agree
Q7.
I feel I have very little control or authority over things that directly affect my job and results. FALSE KEY
Sub-Scale VII — Powerlessness  |  Items: 7*, 19*, 31*  |  All false-keyed (3 items)
1=Strongly Disagree · 5=Strongly Agree
Q8.
My relationships with colleagues or teammates feel unsupportive, tense, or difficult. FALSE KEY
Sub-Scale VIII — Poor Peer Relations  |  Items: 8*, 20, 32*, 41*  |  1 true + 3 false-keyed
1=Strongly Disagree · 5=Strongly Agree
Q9.
My job doesn't give me enough chances to learn new things, be creative, or use my full potential.
Sub-Scale IX — Intrinsic Impoverishment  |  Items: 9, 21*, 33*, 42  |  2 true + 2 false-keyed
1=Strongly Disagree · 5=Strongly Agree
Q10.
I feel my role or position at work doesn't get the respect, recognition, or importance it deserves. FALSE KEY
Sub-Scale X — Low Status  |  Items: 10*, 22*, 34  |  1 true + 2 false-keyed
1=Strongly Disagree · 5=Strongly Agree
Q11.
The physical conditions of my workplace (noise, heat, space, equipment, hazards, etc.) make work tiring or uncomfortable.
Sub-Scale XI — Strenuous Working Conditions  |  Items: 12, 24, 35, 43*  |  3 true + 1 false-keyed
1=Strongly Disagree · 5=Strongly Agree
Q12.
The rewards (salary, benefits, appreciation) I get don't feel fair or worth the effort I put in.
Sub-Scale XII — Unprofitability  |  Items: 11, 23  |  All true-keyed (2 items)
1=Strongly Disagree · 5=Strongly Agree
